About Kigali City

Kigali is the capital city of Rwanda. It is known for being clean, safe, and well organized. Many visitors enjoy Kigali because of its beautiful hills and friendly people.

Why People Love Kigali

  • Clean and green environment
  • Peaceful and safe city
  • Good food and culture

Popular Areas in Kigali

  • Kacyiru
  • Kimironko
  • Nyarutarama
